Welcome to Green Haven Nursery

About Our Nursery

Green Haven Nursery has been nurturing nature since 2010, providing top-quality plants and expert landscaping services. Located in the heart of the countryside, we specialize in sustainable gardening practices and offer a wide variety of plants—from vibrant perennials to sturdy evergreens. Our mission is to bring greenery to every home and business, enhancing lives through the beauty of nature.

Did you know? According to gardening statistics, households with gardens report 25% higher well-being scores. We’re proud to contribute to this by supplying over 50 species of native plants, perfect for local ecosystems!

Our Green Commitment

We’re passionate about sustainable planting. Our nursery uses organic fertilizers and rainwater harvesting systems to grow healthy plants. Fun fact: A single mature tree can absorb 48 pounds of carbon dioxide per year! That’s why we’ve planted over 1,000 trees in community projects last year alone.

Explore our range of eco-friendly services, from custom landscape designs to seasonal plant care tips, all tailored to make your space a thriving green haven.
