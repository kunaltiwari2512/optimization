Our Nursery Services

At Green Haven Nursery, we offer a range of professional services to bring your green dreams to life. From plant selection to full landscape transformations, we’re here to nurture your space.

Plant Nursery & Sales

Browse our extensive collection of over 50 plant varieties, including native perennials, fruit trees, and seasonal blooms. Our plants are grown with organic methods, ensuring they thrive in your garden. Fun fact: A well-placed tree can reduce cooling costs by up to 30% (EPA stats!). Visit our nursery or order online for delivery within 50 miles.

Custom Landscape Design

Transform your yard with our expert design services. We create tailored plans—think butterfly gardens, drought-resistant xeriscapes, or cozy patio greenery. Our team uses 3D modeling to show you the results before we plant a single seed. Last year, we completed 75 unique designs, each blooming with personality!

Plantation Maintenance

Keep your green spaces thriving with our maintenance packages. From pruning to pest control (using eco-friendly neem oil), we’ve got you covered. Did you know regular pruning can increase flower production by 20%? We offer monthly or seasonal plans to suit your needs, trusted by over 200 local homeowners.

Garden Installation & Hardscaping

Elevate your outdoor space with custom installations—raised beds, stone pathways, or water features. Our hardscaping adds structure to your garden’s beauty. Inspired by Japanese Zen gardens, we installed 15 tranquil water features last season alone. Let’s build your perfect retreat!
